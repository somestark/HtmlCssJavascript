Welcome to New Life Midwives

We are situated in the historic district of downtown Bowmanville and offer comprehensive Maternity Care services to low-risk women throughout their pregnancy, labour, birth and early postpartum period. This includes care for both mother and baby during the first six weeks following the birth.

During your pregnancy you and your midwives will have the opportunity to get to know each other well, developing a trusting relationship. Our goal is for you to have a positive empowering experience and we are committed to assisting you in achieving this.

Our team of midwives looks forward to traveling with you on your journey through pregnancy, birth and parenthood.
